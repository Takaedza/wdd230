Birth of Chitungwiza Art Scene

Chitungwiza, a high-density suburb near Harare, emerged as an artistic hub due to limited formal employment opportunities. Many residents turned to visual and performing arts as a livelihood, transforming poverty into creative expression. And exposure to art from childhood, with communities like Unit O in Seke township becoming sculpture hubs, fostered a culture of creativity. Local legends such as musician Alick Macheso and sculptor Sylvester Mubayi inspired generation.
