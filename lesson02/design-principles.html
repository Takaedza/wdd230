Fitt's Law

Google Search

Fitt's law talks about the placement of icons on website to allow ease of use to the site user. This perfectly highlighted by google search which have the search input space at the centre of the website and search button right next to it. If the use want to long in the button is at the top right of the website.

Hick's Law

Amazon

Hick's Law states that the more option a customer is offered the more time they will take to make a decision. To make it easy Amazon predict what the customer want and will show that to ensure they spend less time in making a decision.
