Overview

Site Name

Site will named ChiArt Chamber of Commerce. The name is drived from Chitungwiza which the town it will be focused on. It will focus on the artistic side of commerce hence the "Art" joined with "Chi". The "Chi" is taken from the shortcut used by people to call Chitungwiza.

Purpose

The site will be a commercial chamber link Chitugwiza creative artist with the world and pontetial customers. All the artist from different arrange of the art sector will be invited to become member of the site. The site will act as plateform to keep news of what is happening in the art sector and offer pontetial deals details to its members.

Scenarios

  • What will be the role of the chamber of commerce to help art business with potential investors.
  • What will be the monthly events to beheld to raise awareness for member business.
  • What community project will conducted by the chamber to help uplift the community.
  • What will be the benefit of joining the chamber of commerce to individual businesses with in the community.

Location

The chamber site will be for Chitungwiza creatives.

Audience

Organizations that seek to find different artifacts to enhence their office environment, Investors seeking opportunity in art, Creative individual that seek connection with other creatives, individuals who love art, and government ministry in charge of art.
