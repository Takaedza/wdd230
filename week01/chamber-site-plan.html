Overview

Purpose

The site will be a commercial chamber link Chitugwiza creative artist with the world and pontetial customers. All the artist from different arrange of the art sector will be invited to become member of the site. The site will act as plateform to keep news of what is happening in the art sector and offer pontetial deals details to its members.

Location

The chamber site will be for Chitungwiza creatives.

Audience

Organizations that seek to find different artifacts to enhence their office environment, Investors seeking opportunity in art, Creative individual that seek connection with other creatives, individuals who love art, and government ministry in charge of art.
